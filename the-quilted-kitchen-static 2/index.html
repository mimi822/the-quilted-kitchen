Our Story

We’re a family‑run kitchen inspired by heirloom recipes and that “barn‑quilt” warmth—cozy, colorful, and always from the heart. Each week we bake small‑batch cookies, bagels, loaves, and puff‑pastry treats using local ingredients (like Back to Eden honey!). Come taste the care we stitch into every bake.
